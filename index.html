Guru Arjan Dev

General

Guru Arjan Dev was sent to earth in the year of 1563 on April 15th in Goindval Punjab. His parents are Guru Ram Das and the daughter of Guru Amar Das He is the fifth guru of the sikh religion (learning a religion). He help every body who will not harm him in the future.

Examples Of What He Did

  1. He and the previous Gurus wrote certain banis (Recitations) for certain effects. For example Vazir Khan was plagued with a unknown disease and tried all known cures. Not even one worked until, one day when a started sikh reciting Sukhmami Sahib in front of him. When he stopped the disease got worse.

  2. Completed construction of the Darbar Sahib
  3. Compiled the hymns of previous Gurus and of other saints into Adi Granth
